How to win

EAT ALL THE FRUITS!

MOAR FRUITS = MOAR POINTS!

Controls

When no character is selected, clicking on a character that belongs to your team will select the character if it has AP remaining.

A character can be deselected by clicking outside of the character's movement distance.

Characters

A character initially begins with an Action Point(AP) capacity of 6 and an AP regeneration of 5 per turn.

A player may opt to end the turn without expending all of the AP on a character, thereby allowing more AP to be available on the next turn.

However, any AP that exceeds the maximum capacity will be lost.

Leveling

As you eat fruits, your level increases.

Every level gained increases your maximum AP(AP) by 1.

Every 3 levels gained increases your AP regeneration by 1.

Fruit Spawning

When a fruit is eaten, a new fruit will immediately be spawned.

The fruit will not be spawned on a square that is currently occupied by a character, or another fruit.

Additionally, for every 10 turns elapsed, an additional fruit will be present on the playing field.

The maximum digestion penalty for a fruit will also increase by 1 for every 10 fruits consumed (this total is shared by both teams).
