Rules

Goal of the game

The objective of the game is to eat a specific number of fruits before your opponent to win (20 for the current version).

Character stats

Each character has 5 stats to take into consideration when planning your moves:

Maximum Action Points(AP): This determines the maximum amount of AP this character can have stored at any one time. Any regenerated AP that exceeds this limit will be lost.

Current AP: Determines the number of squares the character can move this turn.

AP Regeneration: Determines the number of AP that will be added (or subtracted!) to the character's current AP at the start of the player's turn.

Level: Increases as the character eats more fruits. Every level gained increases maximum AP by 1, and every 3 levels gained increases regeneration by 1.

Digestion Penalty: A penalty applied to the character's AP regeneration if it eats a fruit that is not ripe yet. This penalty drops by 1 at the start of the player's turn, and cannot go below 0.

Fruits

Fruits are the main objective of this game. Each fruit consumed by a character increases its level by 1, and brings the team closer to their quota.

Initially, only one fruit will be in play at any time. A new fruit will be spawned whenever an existing one is consumed.

Additionally, an additional fruit will be placed on the field every 10 turns, and the maximum digestion penalty for a spawned fruit increases as more fruits are consumed.
